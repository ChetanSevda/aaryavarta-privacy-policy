Privacy Policy – BhajanAmrit

Last updated: March 2026

BhajanAmrit (also referred to as "we", "our", or "us") is a devotional application created to provide bhajans, spiritual videos, and images for users.

1. Information We Collect  
We do not collect any personal information such as name, phone number, or address.

However, the app may automatically collect limited non-personal data such as:
• Device information (model, OS version)  
• App usage data  
• Crash logs and performance data  

This data is used only to improve app performance and user experience.

2. Permissions Used  
Our app uses the following permissions:
• INTERNET – to load content  
• FOREGROUND_SERVICE / MEDIA_PLAYBACK – for background audio playback  
• POST_NOTIFICATIONS – to send notifications  
• WAKE_LOCK – to ensure smooth playback  
• BIOMETRIC / FINGERPRINT – for secure access (if supported by device)  

These permissions are used only for core functionality and not for collecting personal data.

3. Third-Party Services  
We use trusted third-party services such as Firebase for analytics, crash reporting, and performance improvements.  
These services may collect limited technical data as per their policies.

We do not claim ownership of devotional content unless explicitly stated.

If you own any content or have any concerns, you may contact us.  
We will review and remove the content within 72 hours.

The app may store cached images, videos, and audio files on your device to improve performance and loading speed.  
Users can clear cache anytime from device settings.

6. Security  
We take reasonable steps to protect user data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy  
This app is safe for all users and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
